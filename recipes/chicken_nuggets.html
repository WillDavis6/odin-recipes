chicken nugget recipe

Some sweet nuggies in the wild

Nuggies are so yummy yummy, nuggies are so very very yummy yummy

    Ingredients

  • Some fresh raw chicken bums
  • flour
  • bread crums
  • battery acid
  • an oven

    Cooking directions

  1. roll chicken bums in a 95/5 mixture of battery acid and bread crums
  2. set oven to 650 deg farenhite
  3. place nuggies uncovered in oven over night
  4. remove and eat while still hot, dip in flour for flavor!
